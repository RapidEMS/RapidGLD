Most Cars

Traffic lights will be set to the setting that will let the most cars pass. This might not mean the best setting, even for one junction, because junctions do not communicate, and road users on linked lanes might not be able to proceed because the decision about that lane at the next traffic light is different.
Most Cars relieves the most clogged up lanes, but does not take into account how full lanes are in comparisson with others.
